"Quotation Marks" "I love you mom"
Using quotation marks in your search terms lets you search exactly for that word. It means, all yours results will have your search terms in them.
-Dashes dolphins -football
If you want to exclude a term for your search include a hyphen before that word.
~Tilde Music ~classes
Use tilde when you want also its synonyms to appear in the result. The above query will search for music classes, lessons, coaching etc.
